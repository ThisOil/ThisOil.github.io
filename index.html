About Us

About Us

At Wellness and Music, we believe that everyone deserves access to the benefits of music, art and sensory therapy, especially those living with dementia and other challenges. Our mission is to bring joy, stimulation, and comfort to individuals in care homes throughout Aberdeen and Aberdeenshire through our personalized therapy programs.

Our team of highly-skilled and compassionate therapists are trained to work with individuals with dementia and other special needs, providing customized therapy sessions that cater to their unique abilities and preferences. Whether you are looking to improve cognitive function, reduce anxiety, or simply enjoy a creative and engaging experience, our therapy programs are designed to meet your needs.

We understand the importance of providing quality care for those in care homes, which is why we bring our services directly to you. With our flexible scheduling and on-site therapy programs, we make it easy for individuals to access the care they need, when they need it. Join us on our mission to enhance the lives of those in care homes through the power of music and art therapy.

Art Therapy

Art therapy is a powerful tool for individuals living in care homes. It provides a creative outlet for expression and can help stimulate the mind and emotions. Our art therapy program is specifically designed for individuals living in care homes, and is led by trained and experienced therapists. We offer a variety of activities, including painting, drawing, and sculpting, that are aimed at promoting well-being and improving cognitive function. Whether you are a beginner or have experience with art, our program is tailored to meet your needs and abilities.

Come join us and unleash your inner artist!

Music Therapy

Music therapy is a proven and effective form of therapy for individuals living in care homes. It utilizes the power of music to promote emotional, physical, and cognitive well-being. Our music therapy program is led by trained and experienced therapists who use a range of musical techniques, including singing, listening, and playing instruments, to support individuals in their journey to improved health and happiness. Whether you are a seasoned musician or have never played an instrument before, our program is designed to meet your needs and abilities.

Join us and experience the transformative power of music therapy for yourself!

Sensory therapy and massage

Sensory therapy and massage are powerful forms of therapy for individuals living in care homes. They provide a calming and relaxing environment that can help reduce stress, improve sleep, and support overall health and well-being. Our sensory therapy and massage program is led by trained and experienced therapists who use a range of techniques, including aromatherapy, soft lighting, and gentle touch, to create a nurturing and sensory-rich environment. Our massage therapy component is designed to promote relaxation and comfort, and is tailored to meet the individual needs and abilities of each client.

Join us and discover the benefits of sensory therapy and massage for yourself!

Activity Therapy

Activity therapy is a valuable resource for individuals living in care homes with dementia. It offers a fun and engaging outlet for expression, helping to stimulate both the mind and body. Our activity therapy program is specifically designed for individuals with dementia living in care homes, and is led by trained and experienced therapists. We offer a variety of activities, including games, puzzles, and physical movements, aimed at promoting well-being and enhancing cognitive function. Whether an individual is a beginner or has prior experience with activity-based therapy, our program is tailored to meet their needs and abilities.

Our goal is to provide a supportive and enjoyable experience that promotes health, happiness, and a sense of purpose.
